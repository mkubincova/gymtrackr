GymTrackr is a new and inovative way to keep track of your gym experience. After creating an account you can set up your exerices, choose how many set/reps you aim to do and then group them into different sessions, such as "My Monday workout" or "Morning stretch". GymTrackr will let you know when it's time for your workout session, so you can easily plan ahead without worrying that you forget. This amazing app also makes sure you aren't stagnating at one point, but move forward to reach your goal by suggesting more frequent or longer exerices based on your preferences. As an icing on the cake, there is a special treat for smart watch owners, who can user their device to count their reps and time between exercises.
